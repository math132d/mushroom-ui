Mushrooms

Boletus

  • Common name: Boletus (aka, UK - Penny Bun, Italy - Porcini, French - Cep, US - King Bolete)
  • Scientific Classification:
    • Family: Boletaceae
    • Genus: Boletus
    • Species: Boletus edulis
  • Visual Description:
    • The mushroom is easily recognized by its brown cap with a white edge which gets more flattened as it matures.
    • The pores below the cap are white and reminds of a sponge and with age gets more yellow.
    • The stem usually is thick, solid (when young) and tan yellow with a fine net just under the cap, sometimes extending all the way down.
    • Habitat of boletus mushroom is mixed woodland but usually found more with beech, oak or pine.
    • Usually grows in between August and October.
    • Edible, Summer - Autumn
